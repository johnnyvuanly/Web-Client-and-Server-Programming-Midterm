Pokémon TCG

Check whether or not your card is standard legal for tournament play

To see which set your Pokémon card is from, check the bottom of the card for a symbol. This is typically located at the bottom left or bottom right of the card. After finding the symbol, match it with one of the symbols found here and type the set name below. Next to the symbol you'll find a number like "20/214" then enter the first number below. In this case you would enter 20. This means that the card you have is number 20 of 214 from the set.
